Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ZeroQuarry ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, upload source code or binaries, register assets for scanning, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, payment information, and any data you submit to the Service.

We also collect information automatically, including IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and usage data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To analyze assets you submit in order to identify potential vulnerabilities.
  • To communicate with you about the Service, including security alerts and updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. Your Code, Binaries, and Scan Targets

Content you submit for scanning is processed to generate findings, reports, and draft patches. We do not use your submitted content to train third-party foundation models. If you provide your own LLM API key, inference occurs directly against that provider under your account.

4. Sharing

We share information with service providers who help us operate the Service (such as cloud infrastructure and payment processors), with LLM providers when you opt to use ZeroQuarry-managed inference, and when required by law.

5. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, but we continuously work to reduce risk.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Contact us at privacy@zeroquarry.com to exercise these rights.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service or by email.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@zeroquarry.com.
